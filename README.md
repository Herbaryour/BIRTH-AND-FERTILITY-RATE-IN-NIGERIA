#### RESEARCH PROJECT
## ECONOMIC EFFECTS OF BIRTH AND FERTILITY RATE IN WEST AFRICA
# Project Overview:
This research project focuses on analyzing the economic effects of birth and fertility rates across West African countries using statistical models and data analysis techniques.
Objectives
•	Examine fertility and birth rate trends in West Africa 
•	Analyze their economic implications on government resources and development 
•	Identify patterns influencing population growth 
•	Provide data-driven recommendations for policymakers 
Tools Used are Microsoft Excel, Statistical Models, Data Visualization Techniques, Data Interpretation & Reporting

# Key Insights
•	High fertility rates significantly impact economic planning and public resource allocation 
•	Population growth affects employment, healthcare, education, and infrastructure 
•	Proper statistical interpretation can support better government policy formulation 
Impact
The project provides valuable analytical insights that can assist governments, researchers, and policymakers in making informed socio-economic decisions regarding population management and economic development.
